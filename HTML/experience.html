Experience at SP

Freshman Orientation Programme

The freshman orientation program was intended to help ease the students' transition into polytechnic. During the F.O.P, we played ice-breaker games like 2 truths and 1 lie to get to know our classmates better and make new friends. Following this, we had to go through multiple briefings to better understand how poly life was like and how exams were held. Afterwards, we attended multiple talks by CCA groups as they presented to us what their CCA was about and why we should join their CCA. The picture to the right shows the friends I made during the F.O.P.

Sports for life

All year 1 students had to participate in the compulsory sports for life module. For this module, I chose tchoukball. Tchoukball is a sport where 2 sides have to score points by throwing a ball into the frame and make it touch the floor In this sport, interception of the ball is not allowed and players are only permitted to 3 passes before they have to throw the ball into the frame. During this module, I made new friends from different schools in SP. I had fun playing tchoukball and I would love to do something like this again.
